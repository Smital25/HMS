About Hostel Management System

The Hostel Management System is a web-based platform designed to simplify and streamline hostel operations for students, wardens, and administrators.

Our system provides features such as:

  • Room allocation and vacancy tracking
  • Leave request and approval management
  • Attendance tracking and reporting
  • Notification system for important updates

We aim to create a user-friendly experience that improves efficiency and transparency in hostel administration.
